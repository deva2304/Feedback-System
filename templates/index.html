How It Works

100% Real-Time AI Prediction

No static datasets. Everything is captured from your live browsing activity.

🔌
1. Extension Captures
The browser extension detects when you're on an Amazon product page and captures the product name and price.
📡
2. Data Sent Locally
All data stays on YOUR computer. It's sent to your local Flask server — never to the cloud.
🧠
3. AI Predicts
Our engine analyzes click frequency, session duration, price, and return visits to predict purchase probability.
💾
4. Stored Forever
Every interaction is saved to captured_data.csv — your personal browsing dataset grows over time.
